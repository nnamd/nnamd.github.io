## Abstract
 The purpose of the app is in documenting cardiac arrest. In current hospitals there aren’t many if any formal ways to collect this information during codes and it is very often just written on paper which can be lost or misdocumented. Our app solves this by stepping through the formal Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) or Pediatric Cardiac Arrest Algorithm (PALS). I collaborated with 2 medical professionals to work on the app. 

## Technical Contributions
The App Itself was built with the **Flutter Framework and Dart**. The backend was created using **Python** with **FlaskSQL**; a NoSQL database enabled us to do manage user sessions, save information, and in the future pair with organizations. We’re currently hosting the servers for the app on AWS.
