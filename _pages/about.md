## Who Am I?
My name is **Nnamdi Nwagwu**. I am an M.S. Graduate in Robotics. I'm passionate about robots in real-spaces and the functional and social challenges that prevent them. I most recently worked in the SHARE Lab @ Oregon State under Dr. Naomi Fitter where I explored how robots should move and sound to communicate non-verbally. Previously, I graduated with my Bachelors of Science from Cornell University in Mechanical Engineering.
